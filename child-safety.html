Child Safety Standards

Confexy is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse material (CSAM). We strictly prohibit any sharing or promotion of sexual content involving minors.

Measures we take:

  • We monitor and remove any content that violates child safety policies.
  • We provide users with tools to report inappropriate behavior.
  • We cooperate with authorities and relevant organizations when necessary.

If you encounter any violation, please contact us immediately at pedroavcleite@gmail.com.
